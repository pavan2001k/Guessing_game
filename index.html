Harley davidson nightster

The Harley-Davidson Nightster features the new Revolution Max 975T powertrain, getting a 975cc liquid-cooled V-twin engine, pumping out 90PS and 95Nm, mated to a 6-speed transmission. Moving on from its olden days, Harley has equipped this engine with modern bits like variable valve timing, hydraulic valve lash adjustment and counterbalancers.

Benlli 752s

Benelli 752S is expected to launch in India in December 2022 in the expected price range of ₹ 6,00,000 to ₹ 7,00,000. Currently available bikes which are similar to 752S are CFMoto 650GT, Kawasaki Z650 & Kawasaki Ninja 650. Another bike similar to 752S is Benelli TNT600i which is launching in October 2022 in India

Royal Enfield Hunter 350

At the heart of the Hunter beats a fuel-injected, long-stroke 350cc J-engine that delivers a smoother ride, higher RPM and dollops of torque. Paired with a steady downtube spine frame and grounded suspension, the Hunter 350 gives the right amount of power and precision, whether you’re revving up the city streets or gunning down the motorway.

TVS Apache RTR 160

The TVS Apache RTR 160 4V has consistently pushed the limits of performance and technology, always outdoing itself in the race against time. With the first-in-segment Ride Modes and SmartXonnect, the 2021 TVS Apache RTR 160 4V pushes the envelope like never before. Race in any condition with nothing holding you back. It’s time to become the Unstoppable.

Bajaj Pulsar 150

Pulsar 150 promises performance like none other. Enjoy class-leading 10.3 kW (14 PS) power and enhanced 13.25 Nm torque with our patented DTS-i technology.

Honda CB300F

The CB300F is an uncompromising street fighter that is powerful and agile with a masculine toned look that seems straight out of a workout. For a relentless constant performance, the CB300F is equipped with a 4 Valve, 300cc Oil Cooled Engine that generates class leading power and torque.
